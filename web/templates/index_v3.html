GOT QUESTIONS?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about automated job applications

How does the AI application system work?+

Our AI system scans thousands of job listings across multiple search engines and job boards. It analyzes each job description, then crafts personalized cover letters tailored to each specific company and role. Applications are sent through rotating email providers to ensure maximum deliverability. You simply upload your CV, set your preferences, and let the AI handle everything — 24/7, automatically.

Will my applications look like spam or generic templates?+

Absolutely not. Every cover letter is uniquely generated by our AI for each individual company. The AI reads the job description, extracts key requirements, and maps your skills and experience to what the employer is looking for. Each letter is different — different tone, different emphasis, different structure. Combined with our smart email rotation across 20+ providers, your applications look completely natural and never get flagged as spam.

Is there a money-back guarantee?+

Yes! If you don't receive any interview invitations within 30 days of your campaign, we'll refund your full payment — no questions asked. We're that confident in our AI system. Over 85% of our users get at least one interview within the first 2 weeks.

What payment methods do you accept?+

We accept cryptocurrency payments including Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), USDT (TRC-20 & ERC-20), Litecoin (LTC), Solana (SOL), and USDC. Crypto payments offer privacy, speed, and work from anywhere in the world without banking restrictions. All payments are one-time — no subscriptions, no hidden fees.

Which countries and industries do you cover?+

We cover 50+ countries across the Middle East (Lebanon, UAE, Saudi, Qatar, Kuwait, Oman, Bahrain, Jordan, Egypt, Iraq), Europe, North America, Asia, and Australia. Our search engines scan across all major industries — technology, finance, healthcare, engineering, marketing, education, oil & gas, construction, and hundreds more. If there's a job posted online, we can find it.

How is my data protected and my privacy maintained?+

Your privacy is our top priority. Your CV data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your information with third parties. Email providers are rotated to protect your identity. All crypto payments are pseudonymous. You maintain complete control over your data — you can delete it at any time. We don't track you, sell your data, or share anything without your explicit consent.

Can I target specific companies or industries?+

Yes! You have full control. You can target specific job titles, industries, salary ranges, locations, company sizes, and even specific companies. Want to only apply to Fortune 500 tech companies in Dubai paying $80K+? You can do that. Want to cast a wide net across all engineering roles in the GCC? That works too. Our search filters give you complete flexibility.

How quickly will I start seeing results?+

Applications begin within minutes of setting up your campaign. Most users see their first email opens within 24-48 hours, and interview invitations typically start arriving within 5-14 days. The speed depends on your industry, location, and target roles — but our users average an 8-15% interview rate, compared to the industry standard of 2-3%.
