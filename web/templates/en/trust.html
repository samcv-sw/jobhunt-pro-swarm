SECURITY INFRASTRUCTURE

Enterprise-Grade Protection

Your CV, applications, and personal data are protected with the same security used by banks and governments

🔐

AES-256 Encryption

All data is encrypted at rest using AES-256, the same encryption standard used by the US government for classified information. Your data is also encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3.

🛡️

Zero-Knowledge Architecture

Your personal data stays yours. We employ a zero-knowledge architecture where sensitive information is encrypted before it ever touches our servers. Not even our engineers can access your raw CV data.

📊

Automated Backups

Hourly encrypted database snapshots with 30-day retention. Disaster recovery tested every week. Your campaigns and data are always safe and recoverable within minutes.

🕵️

Email Stealth Technology

Our anti-detection system uses human-like timing patterns, rotating email providers, and intelligent warmup to ensure your applications look 100% organic. No spam flags, ever.

🔑

Secure Authentication

Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with per-user salts. Sessions are cryptographically signed and expire automatically. Brute-force protection with rate limiting on all login endpoints.

⚡

DDoS Protection

Protected by enterprise-grade DDoS mitigation. Rate limiting, request validation, and automatic blocking of malicious traffic ensure the platform stays online 24/7.

Download all your data anytime in standard formats. Delete your account and all associated data instantly with one click. No retention tricks, no "cooling off" periods, no hidden copies. When you say delete, we delete — completely and irreversibly.
