Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about JobHunt Pro — your AI-powered job application platform.

What is JobHunt Pro? ▼
JobHunt Pro is an intelligent job application automation platform that helps you find and apply to the right jobs faster. Our AI engine searches across multiple job boards, crafts personalized cover letters, and manages your entire application pipeline from discovery to interview.
How does the pricing work? ▼
JobHunt Pro uses a simple one-time payment model — no subscriptions, no hidden fees. You purchase a tier based on how many companies you want to target. Each tier includes AI-crafted cover letters, email delivery, and pipeline tracking.
What countries do you support? ▼
We support job searches across Lebanon, UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Kuwait, Oman, and remote positions worldwide. Our multi-platform engine searches local and international job boards for the best opportunities.
How does the AI cover letter work? ▼
Our AI analyzes your CV, the job description, and the company profile to generate a unique cover letter for each application. Each letter is tailored to highlight your most relevant skills and experience for that specific role.
Is my data secure? ▼
Yes. Your CV, email credentials, and application data are encrypted and stored securely. We never share your personal information with third parties.
Can I track my applications? ▼
Absolutely. Our dashboard provides real-time tracking of every application. See who opened your email, who responded, and move applications through your pipeline from discovery to interview to offer.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept cryptocurrency payments including Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), USDT (TRC20 & ERC20), Litecoin (LTC), and more. No banking restrictions or exchange rate surprises.
How do I get started? ▼
Getting started takes under 2 minutes. Create an account, upload your CV, choose a pricing tier, and launch your campaign. Our AI handles the rest.
